📌 核心素材档案 · Gabriel Attal

  • 🔹 First knew: 社交媒体 (Twitter/TikTok)
  • 🔹 关键事件: 与Stéphane Séjourné的公开恋情
  • 🔹 感受: 惊讶 → 好奇 → 敬佩
  • 🔹 后续: 搜索新闻，关注法国政治
  • 🔹 人物成就: 法国最年轻总理，公开的性少数政要
  • 🔹 结果: 极其成功，打破传统

✨ 串题心法：名人类直接用本人；非名人类 (朋友/家人/小孩/创业者) 采用“想象/类比/童年故事/精神榜样”，逻辑自洽即可。

⭐ 1. Describe a well-known person you like or admire.名人类 · 直接套用
▼

The well-known person I truly admire is Gabriel Attal, the former French Prime Minister. I first knew him on social media – to be honest, I wasn’t even into politics. But one day a short video popped up about his romantic relationship with another French politician, Stéphane Séjourné. I was surprised because a top-level politician being so open about his private life is rare. Then I became curious and started to search for more about him.

What I admire most is his courage and competence. He became the youngest Prime Minister in modern French history, and at the same time, he never hid his identity. His relationship with Séjourné is well-known yet both remain highly professional. This balance between personal authenticity and public duty really inspires me. He proves that being yourself does not stop you from achieving great success. That's why I like and admire him.

💡 串题要点：直接回答“名人”，强调admiration + 社交媒体初识 + 勇敢与成就。
