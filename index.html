⭐ 高分真实范例 · 现场抽题“拥有成功商业的人” (2025新题)

“I’d love to talk about Elon Musk, a truly disruptive entrepreneur. What impresses me isn’t just his wealth, but his first-principles thinking. When traditional rocket companies charged astronomical prices, he broke down every component to raw material costs and rebuilt the supply chain. That radical mindset turned SpaceX from a joke into NASA’s key partner.
Also, his personal charm is raw and authentic — he sleeps on Tesla’s factory floor during crises and tweets memes about exploding rockets. This authenticity resonates with young engineers. From Tesla to Starlink to reusable rockets, he's redefined what success means. To me, a successful business person is someone who rewrites the rules, and Musk does that better than anyone.”
